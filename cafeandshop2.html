Cafe

In our cosy cafe you can have a rest after a good exercise and enjoy delicious bones and fresh water while your human can have tasty pastries, filling salad and as much coffee or tea as they like!

You can either choose to cool off on the tilefloor or have a soft mat to help your tired muscles to recover.

Sometimes the table next to you might have something paw licking good so please keep your human on a leash while visiting our cafe.

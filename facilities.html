Facilities

Total area for our facilities is about 4 ha. The whole area is fenced with 1.8m high solid fence to ensure that all our adventures friends stay safe.

Forest area (2 ha) is for all the friendly dogs to run free and have fun with their humans and friends. Together we also keep our forest clean. Poop bags and plenty of bins provided by us!
